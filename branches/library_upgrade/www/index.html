Aerith: A Road Trip Slideshow Editor

Welcome! Aerith is a Swing Mashup, or smashup, combining three webservices: Google Maps, Flickr, and Yahoo's geocoding webservice to let users create, view, and share slideshows of their road trip photos. It was presented as a demo at the 2006 JavaOne conference by Richard Bair, Romain Guy, and Joshua Marinacci.

news

June 28th: We have just released the source code to Aerith under the BSD license! We are providing it to developers to learn how these types of effects and 3d integration are done. To get Aerith compiling and running please read the Getting Started guide. This release contains the entire JavaOne demo with the exception of copyrighted images and the mapping component. The mapping component has be moved to the SwingLabs SwingX project where it will be further developed for general use.
